About Our Organization

Al Massria Al Emaratia for Import, Export, and Commercial Agencies was established in 2010 to facilitate global trade with Egypt as a strategic hub. We specialize in importing from global markets into Egypt and exporting high-quality Egyptian products to international destinations, creating valuable commercial opportunities for our global partners.

With our deep knowledge of international markets and extensive global networks, we provide comprehensive services to help businesses navigate the complexities of global trade. Our team of experts offers tailored solutions to connect Egyptian businesses with global opportunities and bring international products to the Egyptian market.

Our mission is to enhance Egypt's position as a gateway for global trade, contributing to the growth and prosperity of Egyptian businesses through strategic international partnerships and innovative trade solutions that link Egypt to markets worldwide.
