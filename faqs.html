Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Egyptian Emirati and our services.

What services do you provide?
We offer import and export services, commercial agencies, consulting, logistics solutions, and specialized training programs to connect businesses across Egypt, the UAE, and international markets.
Where is your company located?
Our main office is located in 10th of Ramadan City, Al Sharqia, Egypt. We also have partnerships and operations in the United Arab Emirates to support our international business.
How can I become a business partner?
You can contact us through our "Contact Us" page or by sending an email to info@egy-uae.com. Our team will get in touch to discuss partnership opportunities.
Do you manage international shipping?
Yes, we provide comprehensive logistics solutions including sea, air, and land freight, customs clearance, and warehouse management to ensure smooth import and export operations globally.
Why choose Egyptian Emirati?
We combine deep regional expertise, strong global partnerships, and years of experience to deliver efficient and reliable trade and logistics solutions, with a focus on customer service and sustainable growth.
