Introduction

At AMAE Store, we are committed to providing a reliable and transparent shopping experience. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to assist you with the return or exchange process in accordance with consumer protection laws.

Return Conditions

  • Return or exchange requests must be made within 14 days of delivery (or 30 days for manufacturing defects).
  • Items must be in their original condition, unused, and in the original packaging.
  • The original purchase invoice or proof of payment is required.
  • Opened or used products cannot be returned unless a clear manufacturing defect is found.

Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns for the following (unless defective):

  • Personal care and skincare products.
  • Customized items or products modified at the customer's request.
  • Consumable goods that have been unsealed.

Refund Process

1

Inspection: We inspect the product upon arrival at our warehouse to ensure its condition.

2

Approval: Return requests are reviewed and approved within 48 hours.

3

Payout: Funds are returned via the original payment method within 7-14 business days.
